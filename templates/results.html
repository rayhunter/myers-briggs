Understanding Your Results

Your MBTI type is determined by your preferences in four key dimensions:

  • Energy Source (E/I): How you prefer to direct your energy and attention
  • Information Processing (S/N): How you prefer to take in and process information
  • Decision Making (T/F): How you prefer to make decisions and judgments
  • Lifestyle Approach (J/P): How you prefer to organize your life and work
